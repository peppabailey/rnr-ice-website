About Us

Founded with a vision to provide clean and reliable ice products, RNR Ice Company serves both commercial and residential customers with top-grade ice blocks, crushed ice, and safe drinking water. Our state-of-the-art facility ensures that all products meet the highest health standards.

Our Story

RNR Ice Company began as a dream shared by three determined sisters — Rochele, Nina, and Raissa. With a passion for business and a commitment to community service, they saw an opportunity in their hometown: clean, accessible ice and water.

In the early days, the trio started small, working out of a converted garage with a single ice machine and hand-delivered buckets. Despite many challenges, their relentless dedication and complementary strengths helped them build a trustworthy brand. Rochele handled operations, Nina focused on customer relationships, and Raissa took care of logistics and expansion.

Today, RNR Ice Company is a thriving local business known for quality and care. Their journey is a testament to what heart, hard work, and a strong bond can achieve.
